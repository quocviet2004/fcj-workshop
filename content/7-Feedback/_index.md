### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment was professional and highly focused. My mentors and colleagues were very approachable and always willing to help whenever I got stuck on challenging or unexpected bugs. One area that could be improved is team interaction. It would be great if the team organized short technical sharing sessions or casual weekend gatherings, as these activities could strengthen teamwork and improve communication and collaboration during projects.


**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentors usually provided keywords, AWS documentation, or guidance on the right approach so that I could explore solutions and troubleshoot issues independently. When a problem was particularly difficult, they would step in and explain it directly. This mentoring approach was especially beneficial for students, as it encouraged proactive learning and helped reinforce knowledge through hands-on problem-solving. In addition, the administrative team was always supportive with paperwork and internship procedures, allowing me to focus fully on developing my technical skills.


**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The work was closely aligned with the knowledge and skills I have acquired in my Software Engineering program. The theoretical concepts I learned in university, such as database design and API workflow design, were applied to real-world projects. More importantly, this internship broadened my perspective beyond traditional server-based systems and introduced me to modern Cloud and Serverless architectures.


**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
In addition to improving my technical skills, I gained valuable experience in following a professional software development workflow. This included learning how to manage tasks effectively, use Git properly for team collaboration, and communicate efficiently between the Frontend and Backend teams to ensure smooth project development.


**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The working environment was open, respectful, and strongly focused on achieving practical results. There was no significant hierarchy between interns and other team members, creating an atmosphere where everyone could communicate and collaborate comfortably. Whenever the system encountered issues or the team needed to meet tight deadlines, everyone worked together to analyze problems and find solutions with a strong sense of responsibility and teamwork.


**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The working schedule was quite flexible, making it easier for final-year students like me to balance internship responsibilities with completing my university graduation project. In addition, having access to a real Cloud environment for hands-on practice and participating in internal training sessions were extremely valuable benefits, allowing me to gain practical experience while reducing the cost of learning.
